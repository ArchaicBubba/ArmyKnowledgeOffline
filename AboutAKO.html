About Army Knowledge Offline

Welcome to Army Knowledge Offline (AKOffline)! Your go to source for any Army link found on Army Knowledge Online. While most (not ALL) links on this website link to Official U.S. Army/Military/Government websites; it is not endorsed or maintained by the U.S. Army. Most official Army and U.S. Government websites are hosted on ".mil" and ".gov" domains. I am SSG Roman Antonacci, I develop, maintain, and run AKOffline. This site is very much a work in progress as there are an oddly large amount of Army websites and will likely change within the coming future as a result. For further information or questions on this please feel free to email them to info@akoffline.us or find me on global. Please note while I do not respond to every email, I do read them. Always, thank you for supporting Army Knowledge Offline.

FAQs

Can I add a website to AKOffline's list of links?

Yes, please submit the website with the fallowing information: Name, Description, Link, Image for site card (if avalible) to the AKOffline github issues page. Once submitted the site will be reviewed. If it is a valid site; it will be added. If any information above is not included it will increase time to add the card.

A site link is incorrect. How do I report it?

Open up an issue on the github page. In the issue, indicate which site is wrong and why the new link is correct.

How long will AKOffline be 'online'?

Current site hosting is payed for until March of 2026.

Did you ever find that Policy letter stating sites like AKOffline shouldn't exist?

No! I'm pretty sure it doesn't exist.

What additions are you planning for the future?

Currently none, The website is currently just being maintained. Any future edits (if any) will be small.

Will you add non-profit links?

No, non-profit links will be add to the site. While non-profits can do a lot of good, there are a lot of sketchy non-profits that target people like veterans. As such there is a blanket; No Non-Profits.
